Why are my Travel Town Free Energy Links Not Working?

If you’ve clicked a Travel Town energy link and didn’t see your lightning bolt balance increase, there are usually three technical hurdles at play:

  1. First, most Travel Town rewards expire within 24 to 48 hours of being issued by the developers.
  2. Second, these links are “single-use” per account. If you’ve already claimed the reward from a Facebook group or a push notification, it won’t activate a second time here.
  3. Finally, make sure that your Travel Town app is synced with your Facebook or Apple ID. If the app is currently running in the background, the reward might not “pop” immediately. You can try force-closing the game and clicking the link again to refresh the game server connection.

How to Manage Your Travel Town Board and Maximize Energy

To progress through your Travel Town itinerary without spending real money, you must become a master of “Board Efficiency.” Instead of clicking every producer immediately, save your energy for high-value auto-orders (like the three Cranes or the Ultimate Jewellery Box). Treat your board like a travel suitcase — organization is key.

By keeping your “Producer” items in the corners and clearing out low-value “Travel Items” quickly, you prevent your board from locking up. Use your free energy links during “Global Travel Events” to double your progress speed. This precise and tactical approach ensures you always have enough resources to unlock the next vacation destination and complete your town’s restoration!

What is Travel Town?

Travel Town is a mobile game released by Moon Active on iOS and Android. It is a merging adventure in which your goal is to merge objects in order to fix a dilapidated town. You can repair the various structures and buildings, with each success bringing the town closer to its former glory. This approach combines merging puzzles with the experience of (re)building a town from the comfort of your mobile device, all packaged neatly into a single casual game.
