Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1️⃣ How often are new free Travel Town energy links released?

New links are released daily and often even multiple times a day. Make sure to check back often as not to miss any because we update them as they come!

2️⃣ Why isn’t my free Travel Town energy link working?

Don’t forget that links can expire after a certain period. Sometimes you may have also claimed the reward. Try kicking your game app before clicking on the link.

3️⃣ Can I claim free Travel Town energy links more than once?

No, you can’t. Each link is redeemable once per player.

4️⃣ Are there cheats for unlimited free Travel Town energy?

No. These gifts are given by the Travel Town game developer. Be very careful with links offering unlimited energy as they are ALWAYS scams.
